Who I Am

I'm a Physical Science undergraduate at the University of Kelaniya with a passion for design, technology, and storytelling. Over 5 years of professional graphic design experience using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop has sharpened my eye for detail, brand aesthetics, and visual communication.
